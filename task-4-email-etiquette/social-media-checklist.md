# RESPONSIBLE SOCIAL MEDIA USE

# DO'S

1. Always use Strong Passwords.
2. Always use Double Authentication.
3. Avoid sharing sensitive personal information online to strangers.
4. Always verify information before acting on it.
5. Limit Screen Time.

# DON'TS
1. Avoid Cyberbullying.
2. Do not accept friend request form strangers.
3. Do not click suspicious links.
4. Do not share private details of friends.
5. Do not use public wifis or use weak passwords.
