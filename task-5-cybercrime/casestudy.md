# UPI/Online Payment Fraud

UPI/Online Payment Fraud is one the largest growing form of fraud in India. It is a form of scam that targets a user's money funds. These scams trick the user into doing unauthorised transactions without them even realising it.

There are a lot of ways in which scam takes place. Some of the most common ways include:
1. Collect Requests : The scammers send money collect requests in the form of prize. The user is tricked and as soon as the pin is entered, the money is deducted.
2. Impersonation : These scammers can act like someone you know, who is in dire need for money. This creates a panicked situation in which many fall and willingly send money to these scammers.
3.Phishing Links : Links masked as golden oppurtunities often fool many users and they lose their hard earned money.

In most scenerios, the older generation is targetted in these scenerios, as they lack the awareness to protect themselves.
The consequences of such scams are dire. Even after money losses, it can ceate a lot of mental trauma, which may lead to many illnesses. It can also do long term losses and lose of trust.


Let us take a fictional scenerio. Shanti Devi, a 70 year old woman receives a call from a random number. As she picks it up, she realises that it's her "grandson" who is calling. According to the caller the "grandson" got into a accident and is in dire need of money to go to the hospital as he is heavily injured. Shanti Devi panicks and quickly sends the described amount without knowing it's a scam.
The issue with such scams is not only the loss of financial funds but also the panic it creates. In this case Shanti Devi was lucky to only lose some money, but these situations can also create a lot of panic, aslo leading to heart attacks.

In conclusion, awareness should be raised for such generation to decrease the chances of such scams happening.
