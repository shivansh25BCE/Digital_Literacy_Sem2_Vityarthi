# STAY SAFE ONLINE

1. Accounts that have a lot of personal info, should be kept private. A student should only accept requests from people that they personally know and that too after veryfying.
2. Two factor Authentication should be enabled for all accounts. This reduces the chance of getting hacked or data breaches.
3. In case of any issues with a service, one should find the customer care numbers from the verified app itself. Google often has many scam sites that may lead to different numbers consequently getting scammed.
4. Never enter UPI pin to receive any payments. Pins are only used while sending money.
5. One should always keep their software updated to decrease the chances of data breaches.
6. Avoid using public wi-fi while doing work that requires you to input sensitive information as such networks often have very less security and may be tapped.
7. A student should be vary of phishing traps like fake internships or job offers. The link should always be verifies before acting on it.
8. A student should be vary of the stuff that he/she speaks online as digital footprint remains permanent and that may cause some issues with future recruiters.
